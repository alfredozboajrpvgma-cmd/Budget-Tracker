# PinkCloud Release Notes

## Version 1.1.0

### Design & Layout Improvements
* **Profile Overhaul:** Redesigned the Profile screen for a cleaner, more spacious layout. We've introduced a left-aligned visual hierarchy to make reading your stats and XP much easier.
* **Current Vibe Enhancements:** The "Current Vibe" widget now features dynamic, beautiful SVG icons (Sun, Cloud, Thunderstorm) depending on your saving habits, replacing the old text emojis.
* **Unified Progress Bars:** The XP progress bar now perfectly matches the elegant style and aesthetic of your budget progress bar on the Home screen.
* **Settings Drawer:** The Settings menu has been moved from the Profile screen to a beautiful, accessible right-side drawer containing everything you need.

### Privacy & Legal Updates
* **Dedicated Legal Pages:** We've replaced the small popup text with full, dedicated pages for our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for much better readability.
* **CCPA / CPA Compliance:** Added comprehensive information regarding California and Colorado Consumer Privacy Rights directly into our Privacy Policy.
* **Account Deletion:** Users can now seamlessly request an account deletion directly from the Settings drawer. The process is fully automated, removing the need to type out your email address manually.

### General Fixes
* **Typography:** Enhanced readability on long text pages with better line height, spacing, and a comfortable maximum reading width.
* **Visual Polish:** Removed bulky cards from menus to let the interface "breathe," fixed background clipping issues on scrolling screens, and smoothed out button styles for a more premium, modern feel.
